Advanced Catalysis Research

Prof. Can Li's group is engaged in interdisciplinary research focusing on both fundamental and applied research in catalysis and making effort to reveal the essential relationship between catalytic performance and the catalyst structure. This group has been tried to understand catalysis at various levels including atomic, molecular as well as nanometer scale, and to apply these understandings to the design and the development of catalysts for practical applications in energy, fine chemical productions, and environmental ecology.

Our Team

Prof. Can Li's group is engaged in interdisciplinary research focusing on both fundamental and applied research in catalysis and making effort to reveal the essential relationship between catalytic performance and the catalyst structure. This group has been tried to understand catalysis at various levels including atomic, molecular as well as nanometer scale, and to apply these understandings to the design and the development of catalysts for practical applications in energy, fine chemical productions, and environmental ecology.

Our team collaborates with renowned researchers including Prof. Gang Ma from University of Dalian, Prof. Gray from University of Illinois, and Prof. Sean from University of Durham, publishing high-quality papers and working to apply academic achievements to real-world applications.
